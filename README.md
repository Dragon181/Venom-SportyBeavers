# Venom X SportyBeavers

Sporty Beavers is a move-2-enjoy game based on AR technology, best free-2-play gaming mechanics, powered by Venom

We believe that there is nothing more valuable than a person's time and that game makers are mercilessly making money off people without giving them anything in return over the long haul

We are creating a space in which time belongs to people, they can spend it having fun, while earning money by playing active AR games and competing with other beavers in the Arena.

Sporty Beavers is a Move-to-Enjoy game based on AR technology, bringing together the best practices of classic Free-to-Play projects and the increasingly popular Play-to-Earn NFT games. Sporty Beavers takes on the task of motivating you to exercise regularly, and onboarding into the complex Play-to-Earn blockchain economy so that you can earn cryptocurrency for engaging physical and gaming activities.

1. Free-to-Play model allows every player to join the SB game, regardless ability to pay for NFT at start
2. Player can earn tokens through competing with other NFT-beavers and selling his NFT-facilities and characters at the internal marketplace
3. AR technology helps us to make physical activity more entertaining (comparing with steps based apps) and to avoid problems with fraud
